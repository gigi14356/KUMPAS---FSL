About the Project

What is Project KUMPAS?

KUMPAS stands for Knowledge and Understanding through Modern Platforms for Accessible Sign Language. It is an initiative by Public Administration students of PUP that promotes Filipino Sign Language (FSL) awareness and education through a dedicated online platform.

🎯

Our Purpose

To introduce basic FSL concepts, teach common signs used in everyday conversations, and promote respect and awareness toward the Deaf community among PUP students.

💻

Self-Paced & Free

Learn anytime, anywhere. Our platform provides continuous access to learning materials — unlike one-time seminars or awareness campaigns.

🤝

Inclusive Communication

We believe no one should feel unheard or excluded because of communication barriers. FSL builds bridges between the hearing and Deaf communities.

📜 Republic Act 11106

Filipino Sign Language is officially recognized as the national sign language of the Filipino Deaf community under RA 11106, affirming its importance in inclusive communication nationwide.

🤟

Filipino Sign Language

FSL is a complete, natural language with its own grammar and structure — used by the Filipino Deaf community to communicate, express ideas, and share culture.

Why FSL Matters

Breaking Barriers Together

Filipino Sign Language is more than just a language — it is a bridge toward a more inclusive, empathetic, and connected Filipino society.

🏛️

Recognized by Law

Under RA 11106, FSL is the national sign language of the Filipino Deaf. Learning it honors this recognition and upholds Deaf rights and dignity.

🌏

Inclusive Communication

About 1 million Filipinos are Deaf or hard of hearing. Knowing FSL allows you to communicate directly and respectfully with members of the Deaf community.

❤️

Empathy & Respect

Learning FSL is an act of empathy. It shows the Deaf community that you value their language and are willing to meet them halfway in communication.

🎓

Education & Equity

For public servants and future leaders, FSL knowledge supports equitable service delivery — ensuring no citizen is left out due to communication barriers.

🤝

Community Connection

FSL connects communities. It opens opportunities for friendship, collaboration, and meaningful relationships between hearing and Deaf Filipinos.

🌱

A Skill for Life

Once learned, FSL stays with you. It enriches your personal and professional life, making you a more compassionate and capable communicator.
